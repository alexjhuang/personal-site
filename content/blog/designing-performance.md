Fast systems do not have to feel frantic. This post walks through the small, deliberate choices that keep performance work invisible to the user while still giving engineers the headroom they need.

## What performance should feel like

- Motion that never blocks intent.
- Data that appears just before someone notices it is missing.
- Feedback loops that close quickly without visual noise.

## Principles I reuse

1. Reduce waiting, not information.
2. Prioritize flow over micro-optimizations.
3. Treat latency as a narrative beat, not a defect.
